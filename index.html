Joe Cycles is a mountain bike shop located in Prazeres, Madeira, Portugal.
Founded by Joselino Sanchez and Jérémy Frotey, it is focused on Downhill, Enduro and Trail. We sell full bikes, frames for custom builds, components, accessories, protective gear, and apparel. We also have a workshop equipped with high-end tools, and operated by the best bike mechanic in Madeira.
In addition, we organize through our Bikulture project bike rentals, guided tours and all-included mountain biking holidays.
